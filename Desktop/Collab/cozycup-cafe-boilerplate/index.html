About Us

Discover the story behind CozyCup Café, where every cup tells a tale. Founded by passionate coffee enthusiasts Bacosa and Galleon, the café was born out of a shared dream to create a warm and welcoming space for people to connect, relax, and enjoy exceptional brews. From sourcing the finest beans to crafting signature blends, CozyCup Café is committed to delivering a unique coffee experience that feels like home. Join us in celebrating the art of coffee, community, and comfort—one cozy cup at a time!
